Alzheimer's Disease

Every year, there are over 500,000 Americans diagnosed with Alzheimer's Disease with an additional 100,000 people dying from the disease globally. Furthermore, Alzheimer's is one of the hardest diseases to diagnose with expert neurologists only able to classify the disease correctly 77% of the time. We provide a novel framework using cutting edge artificial intelligence techniques that can diagnose the disease at its earliest stages using only speaking data with an accuracy of 97%.
